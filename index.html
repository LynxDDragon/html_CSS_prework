More Interesting Things About Me

These are mostly random fun facts, they aren't in any particular order. If you are interested in hearing more detailed stories about these little snippits, you can always private me. And I'll respond if I am not busy.
  • I lived the first 4 years of my life in a corner store. It was kind of odd though. Because it wasn't the typical store. It was like an early concept of a vending machine. There was a window where the client could see everythin on display. And they would just say what they wanted and my grandmother would put it on the counter. So no one ever actually went into the store which also doubled as our house. We had the store section, we also had a kitchen that was also the dining room, A "pila" room, a bathroom, and a singular bedroom where we had like 3 beds... the bedroom was literally a BED room
  • My parents left El Salvador when I was 3. My father left for the U.S. first, tried 3 times before he succeded. My mother soon followed. So they left before my brain could actually store memories. My grandmother was left in charge of me for a year before my parents decided to have me brought to them
  • I was 4 years old when I left El Salvador. I had full awareness of most things that went on by then. So I do recall most of the things that happened as I made my trip out of my home country and into the U.S. My uncle did start out the trip with me. But we got separated half way. They caught him, and he got deported. Out of all the people that were in the group with me, I was the only one that made it into the U.S. I got so many anecdotes about the whole thing, but you all would be reading this for maybe 3 hours.
